Our Journey

2014 – Humble Beginning

Tuhin Sir started with just 5 students in a 250 sq-ft rented room. First batch scored 100% in Class-X Maths.

2015 – First Expansion

Student strength grew to 45. Introduced weekly doubt-clearing sessions & parent-teacher meets.

2017 – Board Success

96% students scored 90%+ in Class-X boards; 3 students ranked in top-10 of WBBSE district.

2019 – Tech Upgrade

Smart-board, simulation software & online test portal launched. Digital notes shared via WhatsApp.

2021 – Competitive Edge

First-ever Class-X board perfect-100 in Maths & Science; 12 students scored 90%+ in both subjects.

2023 – Own Campus

Moved to 3-storey owned building with modern lab, library & AC classrooms. Capacity: 250 students.

2025 – Milestone

1,000+ students mentored (Class 3-10), 98% board-success, 85+ students scored 90%+ in Maths & Science.
